words unsaid.

If eternal return is the heaviest of burdens, then our lives can stand out against it all in splendid lightness.

  —Milan Kundera


A microcosm of eternal return, however, resides in all of us: in the form of words unsaid:

   each,
amorphous,

each,  a molecule
 of our personal diving bells.
