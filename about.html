About Orville Mo-He

You see not

the sadistic

boulder of

Sisyphus;    no,


you see the sun.

That was a bit corny no?

But, in a weird way, I quite like it: even though we instinctively see the sun, when we are made aware of it, we appreciate our holding of that perspective just a little more.

 We hold on to it just a little firmer.

A little story about me:

I was born in the Sychelles to two immigrant Chinese parents without a dollar to their name. I was 2-and-a-half we moved to Belize. We slept in a storage locker on mattresses that hotels had thrown out. In Belize, we were the other. When I was thirteen, I realized the need for a better education so I moved to Miami. My parents couldn't leave the business unattended, so I had to do this alone.

Earlier I said that "I am trying to make the world around me just a little kinder". I say this because even though I am young, I have been fortunate enough to experience being on both sides of the extremes of many spectra. I have been fortunate enough to have had to "eat shit" and not be broken. The world is difficult, so regardless of all those saying the young have it easy, I want to make it easer.

Selfishly, I like wicked problems. I like to think about them, and try to solve them. But that requires cooperation, understamding, trust, and empathy.

On to the fun stuff!

 Yes, I do have a life :P

I am an avid traveler, food enthusiast, and lover of gumbo!

Wandering flea markets of Madrid, seeing the smiles of strangers sitting by the Seine, fishing for piraña in the Amazon, jumping from 18-thousand feet, being the idiot traveler that knows nothing!

This was what taught me the beauty and richness of life.
